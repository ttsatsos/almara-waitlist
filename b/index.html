How it works

  1. Your child checks in. One small thing from their day, on whichever path it touched. It takes about a minute. Nothing is logged without you.
  2. You approve, together. A quick tap from you turns the moment into something shared, and the avatar grows a little.
  3. The letters arrive. Over time, Almara writes your child gentle letters that show them, in their own story, how far they have come.
